About The Chef

Maria Coloca hails from a beautiul island in Goa called Chorao de Graca. She has always been fond of cooking.
Her specialities includes main courses like Sorpotel, Xacuti, Pork Vindaloo, Croquettes and many more.
Some of her signature sweets are the world famous Goan Sweets like Patoleos (steamed sweet dish),
Baath (Goan coconut cake), Bolina (Goan coconut cookies) and Pinagr.

Colaco Delicacies was set up in 2017 as a means to introdice more people to authentic Goan cuisine.
Initially it was just Christmas cakes and sweets, however, the overwhelming response from regulars encouraged
Maria to venture into savoury food and authentic Goan pickles. These recipes hail from her grandmother's kitchen.
